# Singara Chennai Card – User Manual
**SBI × Chennai Metro Rail Limited**
*"The Smarter Way to Move Around Chennai"*

---

## About the Card

The **SBI–CMRL Singara Chennai Card** is a RuPay Prepaid Card issued jointly by State Bank of India and Chennai Metro Rail Limited. It functions as a **"One City One Card"** — usable for metro transit and retail/online payments.

### Card Features
- **Card No.:** Unique 16-digit number printed on the card
- **Valid To:** Last day of the month/year shown
- **EMV Chip:** Contains encoded financial information
- **NFC Sign (")))"):** Enables contactless tap payments at PoS machines, Ticket Vending Machines, Entry/Exit gates

## Usage Guidelines

### 1. How to Activate Your Singara Chennai Card
- Activate at Metro Station counters or designated SBI Branches
- Can also be activated via https://transit.sbi/ or CMRL App

### 2. How to Load / Reload Balance

Top-up can be done at:
- Metro stations (Cash / Debit Card / Credit Card / Prepaid Card)
- Online through Payment Gateway
- Specified SBI Branches (Cash / Transfer from own SBI account)
- UPI (when enabled)

> **Note:** The global (offline/chip) balance gets loaded automatically when balance falls below threshold limit when the card comes in contact with card readers at stations.

**KYC Limits (per RBI guidelines):**
- Minimum Details KYC: up to ₹10,000/-
- Full KYC: up to ₹2,00,000/-

### 3. How to Check Balance

- At any **SBI ATM** → Insert card → Enter PIN → Select "Balance Enquiry"
- Mini statement also available at SBI ATMs
- Online at **https://transit.sbi/** or via **CMRL App**
- At Metro premises on **TOM/EFO counters**

### 4. Offline Transactions

- Card supports offline transactions at chip level
- Global (offline) balance is stored on the chip
- Transactions are processed even without network connectivity

### 5. Usage at Merchant Outlets

- Tap or swipe at PoS terminals showing the NFC/RuPay symbol
- PIN may be required for higher-value transactions

### 6. E-Commerce / Online Transactions

1. Enter card details and CVV on the payment page
2. Receive OTP on your registered mobile number
3. Enter OTP to complete the transaction

> **Note:** E-Commerce transactions use **two-factor authentication** (as per RBI guidelines):
> - 1st factor: Card details + CVV (3-digit number on back of card)
> - 2nd factor: OTP sent to registered mobile number
>
> Ensure your valid mobile number is registered with your Singara Chennai Card.

### 7. Usage at ATMs

- Insert card → Enter ATM PIN
- Available options: Cash Withdrawal (Full KYC cards only), Balance Enquiry, Mini Statement
- Wrong PIN entered 3 times in a day → Card invalidated for the rest of that day

### 8. Metro Rides – Step-by-Step

1. **Tap** the card at the **entry gate** → gate opens → proceed to platform
2. After the journey, **tap** the card at the **exit gate** of destination station
3. Fare is automatically debited from the stored value balance as per ticket fare

> Passes can also be loaded onto the card. While a valid pass is active, fare is **not deducted** as the pass value is recovered upfront.

## Taking Care of Your Card

- Do not hand the card to anyone
- Sign the signature panel on the reverse immediately upon receiving
- Do not write the PIN on the card
- Keep the card away from magnetic fields, direct sunlight, and sharp objects
- Do not bend or scratch the card

---

## Protecting Your Card

- Never share your PIN or CVV with anyone
- Change your PIN regularly via any SBI ATM / https://transit.sbi/ / CMRL App
- Do not use an obvious PIN (e.g., birth date)
- Shield the keypad when entering your PIN at ATMs/PoS terminals

---

## Green PIN

- Set a new PIN via SBI ATM or online portal
- PIN is used for: Cash withdrawals (Full KYC), Balance enquiry at ATMs, PoS payments

---

## Forgot PIN / Duplicate PIN

- Reset via any SBI ATM
- Or visit https://transit.sbi/ or CMRL App
- Or visit the nearest SBI Branch / Metro Station counter

## Lost / Stolen Card

If your Singara Chennai Card is lost or stolen:

1. **Immediately** contact the Metro Counter or call the Bank's helpline:
   - **1800-1234** / **1800-2100** (Toll Free)
   - Or block via the customer portal
2. Request a **replacement card** at:
   - Ticket Issuance Counter of any Metro Station
   - Designated SBI Branches (Full KYC cards)
3. Replacement card fee: **₹100**
4. Transit/Metro (Global) balance transferred to replacement card within **7 working days**

## Terms and Conditions (Key Points)

- **PIN** must be safeguarded. Wrong PIN 3 times/day invalidates the card for that day.
- **Bank's records** are conclusive and binding for all transactions.
- **No interest** is payable on unutilized balances.
- Card is **valid only in India**.
- Bank can approve/reject any transaction at its discretion.
- Cardholder must not countermand an order placed via the card.
- Bank is not liable for losses due to technical breakdown of the payment system.
- Cardholder must collect and preserve transaction receipts from Merchant Establishments.
- In case of **cardholder's death**, account operations stop; balance settled as per deceased constituent rules.
- Bank has the right to amend terms at any time; changes communicated **one month prior** via website or other means.

### Refund at Card Surrender
- Retail (Host) + Transit/Metro (Global) balance refunded within **7 working days** by the Nodal SBI Branch.
- Refund request must be processed by **Nodal SBI Branch only**.
- Bank may debit offline transactions not recorded at time of surrender.
